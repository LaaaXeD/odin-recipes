Odin Pizza

Margherita Pizza

Bubbly crust, fresh tomatoes straight from the vine, stringy mozzerella cheese, fresh basil with a spritz of olive oil. A satisfying meal that will satisfy all!

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups (300 g) unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon active dry yeast or SAF instant yeast
  • ¾ teaspoon Diamond Crystal kosher salt
  • 7 ounces (105°F to 115°F) warm water
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • semolina and all-purpose flour for dusting the pizza peel

Steps

  1. Prepare pizza dough
  2. Scrape the dough onto a well-floured counter top and knead the dough for three minutes
  3. Cover the bowl with a kitchen towel (or plastic wrap) and allow the dough to rise in a warm, dry area of your kitchen for 2 hours or until the dough has doubled in size
  4. Preheat Oven
  5. Assemble the ingredients
  6. Separate the dough into two equal-sized portions
  7. Assemble the Pizza
  8. Eat up!
Enjoy!
